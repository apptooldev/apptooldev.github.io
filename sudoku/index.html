Features

Everything you need to truly master Sudoku

Not just another puzzle app. A complete learning system built around the moment of understanding.

💡

Step-by-Step Hints

When you're stuck, get the next logical move — with a clear explanation of the reasoning. Not just the answer, but the why behind every step.

📖

Technique Tutorials

Learn classic strategies at your own pace, from basic elimination to advanced logic. Build a real solving toolkit, one technique at a time.

🔄

Solution Replay

Once you finish, replay the entire puzzle step by step. Turn "I got lucky" into "I know exactly what I did."

📊

Four Difficulty Levels

Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert — pick your challenge and push your limits whenever you're ready for the next step.

✏️

Custom Puzzles

Spotted an interesting puzzle in a magazine? Enter it manually and start solving instantly, anywhere, anytime.

⚙️

Smart Assistance

Notes mode, duplicate highlighting, and auto-remove candidates — so you can focus on thinking, not on housekeeping.
